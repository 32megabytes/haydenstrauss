Hi! I'm Hayden Strauss. I'm a photographer based in Boston, MA.

I have roughly a decade of experience in photography; working in portraits, fashion, events, advertising, photojournalism, fine art wedding, and more. Growing up in Seattle, much of my work centered around the overlooked details in my environment. I documented the abandoned buildings and forgotten corners of the city. This focus translated into my work as a fine art wedding & event photographer, where capturing fleeting moments was key to perserving memories for my clients. Currently, I'm attending college in Massachusetts and have withdrawn from photography as a full-time occupation. That being said, I'm still highly active in the field and continue to work on a weekly basis.
